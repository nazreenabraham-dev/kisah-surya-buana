The Seven Mysteries of Malaya
The Complete Tale of The Nusantara Mistik Cases
By Nazreen Abraham Stein

"A pulp fiction meant to be read over your cekodok and teh ais ikat tepi.
Grease these pages up. Make it wet."

What began with the case of a lost black cat, Bulat. This fantastical journey follows the broken-hearted Johan Anwar, being dragged by the eccentric man with many names, Surya Buana, as they try their best lah to solve the X-files plaguing the Malay Peninsula.

Read how a group of outkasts stumbled their way across the shadows of our nation's secrets. Find out how an adventurous feline time travels to the 1950s — and what role this kucing played in the composition of our national anthem and the declaration of our Merdeka.

Tragically, it identifies itself as a comedy.
